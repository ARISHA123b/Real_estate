* **Austin, Texas:**
    * **Price:**  High, due to rapid growth and limited supply.
    * **Features:** Booming tech sector, high disposable income, strong millennial and Gen Z demographics, preference for experiential retail, limited high-quality retail space. 
* **Raleigh, North Carolina:**
    * **Price:**  Moderate to high, driven by job growth and a diverse population.
    * **Features:** Thriving Research Triangle Park, attracting young professionals, diverse and growing population, focus on education and healthcare, strong local economy with low unemployment and high median household income.
* **Nashville, Tennessee:**
    * **Price:**  Moderate, with potential for appreciation due to rapid growth.
    * **Features:**  Rapidly growing music and entertainment industry, strong healthcare sector, expanding corporate presence, emerging retail corridors with high development potential.
